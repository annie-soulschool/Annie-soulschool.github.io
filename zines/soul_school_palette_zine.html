4 The Rules
Gold is always primary
All CTAs, buttons, and key moments stay gold. Pops support.
One pop per section max
Never mix two accent colors in the same viewport. Hierarchy collapses instantly.
Each pillar owns a color
Five pillars, five pops. Consistent across every page, every context.
Pink = love, always
Reserved for love-coded moments: Liberation, Joy, heart imagery.
Dark tones absorb into void
Dark bg versions fill cards. Bright versions are for text and borders only.
Never pure white or black
#F3F1EB not #FFF · #0A0A08 not #000.
5 Colors that compete with gold
Pure white — too cold, kills the warmth
Pure black — too harsh, loses the depth Void Black gives
Bright yellow gold — flat and cheap, makes Signal Gold look like a highlighter
Dark goldenrod — muddy, fights Ancient Gold and loses
