What is Soul School?

Soul School is a living educational playground for curious humans who are ready to grow, connect, and build new ways of learning together.

How Access Works

The best way to enter Soul School is to show up.

In a world engineered for distraction, showing up is RADICAL. Above all, we show up, for ourselves, each other, and our planet.

  • Attend a gathering.
  • Meet the community.
  • Look for the Soul School bus.

Participants at events may receive the access code.
